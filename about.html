About This BMI Calculator

This website provides a free and simple BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator designed to help users quickly estimate their BMI based on height and weight.

The tool is easy to use, works directly in your browser, and does not require registration or downloads.

Our goal is to offer a fast, accessible, and informative online calculator for educational and informational purposes only.

BMI is a widely used indicator to classify underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity categories.
